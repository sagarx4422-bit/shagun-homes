Helping Families Find Their Perfect Home Since 2012

Founded in 2012 by Ajay Patil, Shagun Homes started with a simple belief: finding a home should be an exciting journey, not a stressful ordeal. Operating out of our first office near Chakki Naka in Kalyan East, we set out to change how real estate consulting works.

Over the last decade, we have proudly assisted more than 500 families in finding their dream homes across Kalyan and Dombivali. Our deep local knowledge and strong relationships with top builders allow us to offer unparalleled access to the best properties.

At Shagun Homes, we believe in complete transparency. We ensure every recommended project is legally verified. Personal, honest, and reliable service is our promise to you.
